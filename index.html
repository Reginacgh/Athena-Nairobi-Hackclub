Athena Nairobi Club

Athena nairobi is a club where we organize hackathons a full day coding marathon where participants collaborate on projects, develop news skills and enjoy the experience. we welcome teenagers who identify as girls or gender non conforming youth to join us for a full day of learning and fun!!

workshops and tracks

build your own video game

in this workshop participants will learn to create 2d video games in javascript. they will use sprig, a web based javascript editor created by hackclub . after this students complete this track they will be shipped a free hand held console to play their game on

create your own website

In this workshop participants will learn HTML and CSS by building their own website. they will use replit and online code editor to build and host their site. with this track attendees will learn the building blocks of web Development.
